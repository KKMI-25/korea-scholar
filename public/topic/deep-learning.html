About Deep Learning Research

Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses multi-layered neural networks to model complex patterns in data. It powers breakthroughs in image recognition, speech processing, natural language understanding, and generative AI.

K-Scholar indexes deep learning papers from over 250,000 journals and repositories worldwide, providing free and instant access to both open-access and preprint research.

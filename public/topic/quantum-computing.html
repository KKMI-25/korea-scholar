About Quantum Computing Research

Quantum computing leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to process information in fundamentally new ways. Research spans hardware (superconducting, photonic, trapped ion), algorithms (Shor's, Grover's, variational), and applications in chemistry, optimization, and cryptography.

K-Scholar provides free access to quantum computing papers from arXiv, IEEE, Nature, and hundreds of international research journals.

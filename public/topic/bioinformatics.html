About Bioinformatics Research

Bioinformatics combines biology, computer science, and data analysis to understand biological data at a molecular level. The field is central to genomics, personalized medicine, and the development of new therapeutics.

K-Scholar makes thousands of open-access bioinformatics papers available for free, covering leading journals such as Bioinformatics, PLOS Computational Biology, Nucleic Acids Research, and many more.

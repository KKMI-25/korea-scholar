About Climate Change Research

Climate change research encompasses the scientific study of long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. Researchers across earth sciences, environmental engineering, ecology, and policy studies contribute to understanding and addressing the climate crisis.

K-Scholar aggregates climate research from open-access repositories worldwide, making it easy for scientists, students, and policymakers to access the latest findings without paywalls.
